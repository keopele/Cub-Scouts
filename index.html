About Our Pack

Pack 2008 is a family-volunteer-lead Cub Scout pack based in Cedar Park, TX. We welcome boys and girls in grades K–5 to participate in fun, skill-building activities that promote character, citizenship, and leadership.

We meet weekly and hold exciting events like camping, Raingutter Regatta, Pinewood Derby, and service projects.

Join the Adventure

We are actively recruiting! If your child is in Kindergarten through 5th grade, they’re eligible to join Cub Scouts.
